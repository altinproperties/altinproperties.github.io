Why "The Haven"?

Actual Site Tour Video

“The Haven” is a premium villa project in Kottayam by Altin Properties, which is only 1.7 kms away from Kanjikuzhy. The project is located in the most sought-after residential area in Kottayam, which is evident from the number of the apartments (approx. 450) within quarter-a-mile radius from the location of “The Haven”. “The Haven” is just a couple of minutes’ drive from Kanjikuzhy, the most-happening place in Kottayam. It is strategically located near Indira Nagar, Devalokam which makes it an ideal place to live, closer to the best shopping venues & schools in Kottayam. The project is located midway of the Municipality Jubilee Road that connects Devalokam to Thuruthel Palam on Kanjikuzhy – Puthupally Road (State Highway – 9).

“The Haven” is undoubtedly one of the luxurious and low cost villa projects for sale in Kottayam, which consists of 12 contemporary villas and a Clubhouse. It offers 3BHK, 4BHK & 5BHK villas with unparalleled features. These premium villas in Kanjikuzhy offers the best ever sold specs at Kottayam. High demand for residential units around the project location is driven by its proximity to the major schools in Kottayam - Pallikoodam High School, Marian High School, Mar Baselios School etc. and shopping centers and hospitals in Kanjikuzhy.

Though the project location is just over half-a-kilometer (approx. 600m) away from Kanjikuzhy – Puthupally Road (SH – 9), it is a very quiet and scenic place overlooking lush green fields, offering mind calming and refreshing vistas from the site.
